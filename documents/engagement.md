## Our Process

### 1. Discovery
Initial conversation to understand your needs, constraints, and goals. We discuss technical requirements, timeline, budget, and compliance needs.

### 2. Proposal
Detailed proposal including:
- Scope of work
- Technical approach
- Timeline and milestones
- Investment

### 3. Engagement
Contract execution on 1099 basis. Regular check-ins and transparent communication throughout the project.

### 4. Delivery
Production-ready deliverables with documentation. Knowledge transfer to ensure your team can maintain and extend the solution.
